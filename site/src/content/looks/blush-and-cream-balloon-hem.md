Auntie duty called this weekend, and this is what showed up: the pants
that look fancy but feel like pajamas, a bodysuit the color of frosting,
and flats I can chase a four-year-old in.

The trick here is the scarf at the waist: it's doing the work a belt
usually does, but softer. One printed piece against all that cream and
suddenly the whole thing looks *decided*, not defaulted.
