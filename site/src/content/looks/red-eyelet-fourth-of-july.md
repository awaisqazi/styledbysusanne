Every July I watch people panic-buy a flag shirt they will never wear
again. Here's the move instead: one red dress, great texture, zero
gimmicks. It photographs beautifully at the cookout and works again at
a summer wedding welcome party, date night, and next year too.

The eyelet fabric is the quiet hero: it gives the bold color somewhere
to live so it feels rich instead of loud.
